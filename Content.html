Glossary

  • Roglelike: A game where if your character is killed you restart from the start of the game losing all your progress
  • Roguelite: A game where if your character is killed you restart from the start of the game but are able to obtain upgrades to your character to make them more powerful
  • Indie: A game developed by a small group of people usually having around 10 people
  • AAA: A game developed by a large team of developers which can be thousands of people all working on the same game
